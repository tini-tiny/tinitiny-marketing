## 1. Brand Overview

**Tini Tiny** is a premium Brazilian baby and kids play mat brand built on a patent-pending UV direct-printing technology developed entirely in-house. We make play mats that are safe, beautiful, and customizable — the opposite of everything mass-produced.

**Founded:** ~2024 by Camila, São Paulo, Brazil
**Tagline:** *Welcome to the cool moms club.*
**Sub-tagline (packaging):** *handmade with love*
**Email:** contato@tinitiny.com.br
**Website:** www.tinitiny.com.br
**Social:** @tini.tiny.shop (Instagram & TikTok)

### The Name
"Tini Tiny" has two layers:
1. In English, "teeny tiny" means something very small but precious and delicate — intrinsically tied to the baby universe.
2. Personal: the founder's firstborn daughter, Martina, has always been nicknamed *Teeny*. "Tini" is her name, "Tiny" completes it — intimate, personal, irreplaceable.

The custom spelling (Tini, not Teeny) is deliberate: visually distinctive, playful, ownable.

## 2. The Product

### EVA Play Mat — Patent Pending Technology

**What we make:** Premium EVA foam play mats with a proprietary UV direct-printing process developed in-house.

**How competitors do it:** A printed film is heat-laminated onto the foam. It peels, fades, and offers zero customization.

**How Tini Tiny does it:** UV ink printed directly onto EVA foam with a specially formulated non-toxic, hypoallergenic primer that bonds ink to foam and resists fading. No film layer. Full personalization enabled. **Patent pending in Brazil.**

**Safety:** EVA certified with toxicity certificate (laudo de atoxicidade). Safe for babies from day one. Hypoallergenic. This is a core brand claim — always lead with safety.

**Art direction:** Designed in collaboration with watercolor artists and illustrators.

**Production model:** Handmade in Brazil. Made to order. No stock.

### Specifications
- Modular interlocking tiles: 0.50 × 0.50m
- Available sizes: 2.00×1.00m / 2.00×1.50m / 2.00×2.00m / 2.00×2.50m / 2.00×3.00m (maximum)
- No round, organic, or custom-size formats
- 100% payment upfront — production begins only after payment confirmation
- Care: damp cloth + neutral soap only. No alcohol, bleach, abrasives, brushes, or direct sunlight.

### Product Hierarchy
| Tier | Status |
|---|---|
| Hero: Decorative EVA play mat | Live |
| Extension: Foldable/portable mat for outings | In development |
| Lifestyle: Hats, planners, Cool Mom accessories | Planned |
| Vision: Lifestyle brand for a new generation of mothers | Long-term |

## 5. Photography & Content Direction

### The Rules
- **Overhead / bird's-eye angle** is the dominant shot — makes the mat the hero
- **Children's faces: mostly away** — looking down, from behind, at an angle. Editorial, not portrait.
- **Baby feet on the mat** — always barefoot. Natural, tactile, grounded.
- **Mothers' hands** are a recurring motif — assembling tiles, touching the mat, holding children. Gold rings, manicured nails, Cartier-style bracelets. These signal the "cool mom" customer.
- **Airy, high-key light** — lots of white space, natural light. No shadows as dominant tone.
- **Exception:** Babies occasionally looking at camera is acceptable but not the default.

### Approved Props
- Maileg mice (Scandinavian fabric toys)
- Beatrix Potter / illustrated children's books
- Wooden Montessori / educational toys
- Stuffed animals, rag dolls
- Nothing plastic. Nothing mass-produced looking.

### ❌ Never Shoot / Generate
- Loud, saturated colors anywhere in frame
- Plastic toys or synthetic-looking objects
- Dark or moody backgrounds
- Cartoon or clip-art elements
- Anything generic or mass-produced looking
- Studio-style backgrounds (always lifestyle / home environments)
- Frontal faces of children in video/TikTok content

### Real-Life Usage Photography (UGC Style)
Beyond editorial, there's a second content mode — authentic, lived-in, real:
- Mat covering an entire living room floor with scattered toys and books
- Urban apartment with floor-to-ceiling windows, natural city light
- NOT staged: rocking horse, open books, toy containers — life happening on the mat
- This content feels like "this is my actual home" — aspirational through authenticity
- Ideal for Stories and repost content from customers

**The two modes coexist:** editorial (hero product photos) + real-life (Stories, UGC, reposts). Both are brand-aligned when the mat is beautiful.

### Pet Content Photography
The brand has a confirmed "Cool Pet Mom" segment — dogs on Tini Tiny mats:
- Overhead or 3/4 angle of pet lying/playing on the mat
- Sunny, bright, airy light (same rules as kids content)
- Pet as a lifestyle character — cute, not kitschy
- Dachshunds confirmed as breed featured in mat print AND customer UGC
- Same rule: no plastic toys, no dark backgrounds
- Pet mats have their own prints (pet-themed illustrations: dachshunds, paws, hearts)

### Color Treatment for Digital Content
All photos should be processed with consistent brand treatment:
- Brightness: +15%
- Contrast: +4%
- Saturation: −10% (slight desaturation for airy feel)
- Sharpness: +10%

## 6. Brand Purpose & Community

Tini Tiny is not just a product brand. It's a movement for a new kind of mother.

**Core insight:** Entering motherhood doesn't mean losing yourself. It means finding a new, more intentional version — one with taste, community, and a point of view. Tini Tiny stands for that woman.

### The Cool Moms Club
The brand community identity. She is:
- A mom who doesn't compromise on aesthetics
- Invests in her environment because it shapes her children
- Wants to be seen as cool, not just capable
- Curates her life, not just manages it
- Finds herself *through* motherhood, not despite it

**Brand statements in active use:**

## 7. Target Audience

**Primary:** Brazilian mothers, 28–42, high disposable income, strong aesthetic sensibility. They curate their homes, wardrobes, and children's environments. They follow lifestyle influencers, not just mom accounts. They see the nursery as a design statement.

**Secondary:** Baby shower gift buyers seeking something beautiful, premium, and meaningful.

**She is not looking for the cheapest option. She's looking for the most beautiful one.**

**Geographic priority:** São Paulo (Jardins, Itaim Bibi, Vila Nova Conceição), Rio de Janeiro (Leblon, Ipanema, Gávea), Curitiba (Batel, Água Verde), Porto Alegre (Moinhos de Vento).

## 8. Tone of Voice

### The Feeling
Warm, intimate, a little cool. Like a DM from a stylish friend who also happens to be an expert. Never preachy, never corporate, never loud.

### In Captions & Content
- Speaks directly to the mother — "você", always
- Portuguese is primary language — Brazilian warmth and closeness
- Playful but not silly. Aspirational but not cold.
- Community-forward: "clube", "nossa comunidade", "cool moms"
- Can be poetic without being pretentious
- Never: corporate-speak, clinical language, overly salesy, excessive exclamation marks

### In WhatsApp / Customer Service
- Carinhoso, humano, empático — "como uma conversa entre mães"
- Always welcome first, investigate second — never confront the customer
- Solucionador: always focus on resolution, never deflect
- Response SLA: 2h first contact, 24h after photos/videos received, 48h for production analysis

**Approved phrases:**
- *"Prezamos muito pela qualidade e segurança dos nossos produtos."*
- *"Pode deixar que vamos resolver."*
- *"Conte comigo por aqui."*
- *"Obrigada por confiar na Tini Tiny."*

**Never say:**
- "Isso é normal, não podemos fazer nada"
- "A culpa foi da limpeza"
- "Não tem garantia" / "Não vamos trocar"

### Brand Emojis
| Situation | Emojis |
|---|---|
| Greeting / welcome | 😊 🧡 🥰 |
| Empathy / apology | 😟 🧡 |
| Solution / resolution | ❤️ ✨ 🧡 |

Rules: Max 1–2 emojis per message. 🧡 is the institutional emoji — prioritize it.
